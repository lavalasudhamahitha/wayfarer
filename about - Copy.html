About Wayfarer

Wayfarer is an AI-powered travel planning platform designed to help you explore the beautiful destinations across India.

Our intelligent chatbot creates personalized travel itineraries based on your preferences, budget, and interests. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, cultural experiences, or spiritual journeys, Wayfarer helps you plan the perfect trip.

Features:
  • AI-powered trip planning
  • State and district-wise destination selection
  • Day-by-day itinerary generation
  • Hotel booking assistance
  • Budget planning and management
  • Interactive AI chatbot for travel queries

Note: This is a Diploma Final Year Project developed for educational purposes.
